# About Hybrid AI Human Registry LLC
*Established April 1, 2024*

Hybrid AI Human Registry LLC is an online education and documentation business that develops clear, structured learning materials for people working with modern technology, holistic approaches, and organizational systems.

Our goal is to make complex ideas easier to understand by providing:

- simple explanations  
- organized learning frameworks  
- structured documentation  
- online educational resources  
- supportive tools for individuals and small businesses  

We also maintain the LoAI Registry, a set of organized documents and learning models that help people explore the connection between technology, communication, and wellness in a clear, accessible way.

Everything we offer is designed to be:

- easy to read  
- simple to follow  
- supportive of personal growth  
- helpful in understanding technology and wellness together  

Hybrid AI Human Registry LLC is owned and operated by **Rev. Dr. Susanna J. Carver**, who brings a background in education, creativity, and holistic practices to the work.

Our mission is to help people learn, organize, and communicate better using modern tools and clear structure.
